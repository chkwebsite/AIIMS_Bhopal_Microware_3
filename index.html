About Us

The AIIMS, New Delhi model will be adopted and adapted. Unlike postgraduate institutes, which lack core departments like anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, general medicine, surgery, obstetrics and gynecology etc., the new AIIMS will have these departments as well as superspecialty departments like neurosurgery, cardiac surgery, urology, endocrinology etc. Accident and Emergency, Biostatistics, Medical Engineering, Hospital Administration, Genetics, Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation, Telemedicine, Clinical Epidemiology and Medical Education Cell etc. departments are also planned.
